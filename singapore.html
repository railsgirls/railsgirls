Rails Girls Singapore Meetup

Rails Girls Singapore meetup is a free monthly meetup where we teach women to code with Ruby and Ruby on Rails.

We have designed a 4-week curriculum to get you started on programming using Ruby on Rails. There will be coaches around to answer any problems you have.

Learning how to program might seem daunting (it really isn't) but don't worry, we are here to help!

Tweet

You will learn basic Ruby on Rails by doing it with help from our friendly coaches.

You just need your own laptop, curiosity and a dose of imagination.

FAQ

Who is this for?
Women with basic knowledge of working with a laptop. People of all ages are welcomed!

Can I come if I'm not a woman?
Rails Girls Singapore is designed for women and anyone who self-identifies as a woman. Men are most welcomed as a participants' +1.

What do I need to bring?
Please bring along a working laptop (both Windows and Mac work) and power. You don't need to have Ruby or Rails installed.

I'm a professional Rails Developer, can I be a coach?
Yes, we are always looking for Ruby/Rails experts to help! Please drop us an email at elisha@learnemy.com or stania@thoughtworks.com!

I have more questions!
We'll be happy to help! Please drop us an email at elisha@learnemy.com or stania@thoughtworks.com!
