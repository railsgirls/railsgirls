FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. People of all ages are welcome to Rails Girls events. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, women are given a priority.
