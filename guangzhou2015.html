FAQ

How much does the workshop cost?（需要支付费用吗？） Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!（本次活动完全免费，只要带着一颗分享的心）

Who is this aimed for?（参加的对象） Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop.
我们欢迎任何年龄段的女生，只要你对电脑有基本的了解。同时，请带上你的笔记本电脑。

Can men attend?（我是男生，可以参加吗？） Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
