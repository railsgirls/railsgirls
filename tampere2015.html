Rails Girls Tampere 25-26 September 2015

Rails Girls comes to Tampere again! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails. No programming experience is required. The workshop will be held in Finnish and participants must be 18 years or older. The application period is open until August 31st.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of 18 years or older with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop. Workshop will be held in Finnish!

Can men attend? Yes, but girls are given a priority.
