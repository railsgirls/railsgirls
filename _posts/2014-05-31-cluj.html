FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to apply. All you need is your laptop ;)

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in Romanian, but small group work can be done in English if needed.

Can men attend? Women are given priority, however, if you bring a girl with you we will be glad to invite you.
