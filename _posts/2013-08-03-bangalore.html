Rails Girls Bangalore 3rd August 2013

Namaskara world!

Rails Girls comes to Bengaluru on Saturday, August 3rd! During the one-day workshop for women, we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails. This free event is for beginner women who want to learn more about coding and building things.

Follow #RailsGirlsBLR on Twitter for the latest updates!
