Partners

Rails Girls Bangalore is co-organized with our awesome partners.

Want to help? We're looking for partners & sponsors for the non-profit event! Email us!

Simpl is a technology company that works with merchants and financial institutions to enable a world where every monetary interaction is effortless and transparent. The platform is built with RoR, Python, GoLang, and NodeJs. The underwriting, fraud monitoring and UX are powered by machine learning.

Maven Hive is a Bangalore based consulting firm specializing in end-to-end product development and training. They have built a team of talented people from diverse backgrounds who have come together for one mission, to "turn ideas into awesome software"! Over the last 6+ years, they have worked with several startups and organizations from across the world realize their product vision and help them achieve major milestones in their journey.

Quintype is a media technology company based in Bangalore. The company is known for its Quintype Publishing Platform, a Headless CMS that allows publishers and content marketers to publish, analyze, and monetize content in a cloud, fully integrated platform. Quintype is a full-stack SaaS platform based Content management system which includes front-end and back-end development and also offers developer services.

Progate is an online platform for learning to code. The key idea behind the tool is to allow even complete beginners to learn without frustration. Many people want to start programming, but don't know how or where to start. Their goal is to solve this problem by making the world's best service for learning to code and delivering it to beginners all over the world.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
