Rails Girls The Hague

Hello The Hague!

Rails Girls will land in The Hague to help aspiring lady techies build an application. We can't wait to dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails with you!

We'll open applications early July this year. Follow us on Twitter via @RailsGirls_DH!
