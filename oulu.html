FAQ

How much does the workshop cost?
Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for?
Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work can done in Finnish. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend?
Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
