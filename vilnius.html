Rails Girls Vilnius October 10-11, 2014

Rails Girls is coming back to Vilnius! During this free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails. Join the Internet playground, no experience required – just some curiosity and a laptop!

Applications are closed.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work is done in lithuanian. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
