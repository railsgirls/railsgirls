Rails Girls Kansas City November 2nd and 3rd, 2018

Rails Girls comes to Kansas City! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of building web applications with Ruby on Rails.

Applications are currently closed.



Tweet

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

Want to help? We are looking for volunteers and coaches. Please apply here.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Anyone is welcome but women and girls are given priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us

What's your code of conduct? Rails Girls Kansas City is operating under the umbrella of Kansas City Women in Technology and operates under the same code of conduct
