Rails Girls SLC21-22nd March 2014

Rails Girls comes to Salt Lake City, Utah! During the free two-day workshop we’ll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

This workshop is intended for people new to programming, or new to Ruby on Rails. No prior experience is required. Questions? Email Anna at anna@bigcartel.com.

Apply now! Applications are open until February 28th, and space is limited. We'll notify you of your application status by March 7th.

Want to help? We are looking for Rails coaches.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it’s free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.
