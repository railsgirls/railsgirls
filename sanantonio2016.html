Rails Girls San Antonio January 22-23, 2016

Hello world!

Rails Girls comes to San Antonio! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

Applications are open until the 10th of January 2016.

This workshop is intended for people new to programming, or new to Ruby on Rails. No prior experience is required. We have room for 20 participants, so get your application in early! Questions? Email one of our organizers (listed in the bottom of the website)

Web developers that want to help coach / teach, please contact Katarzyna Szawan, kat.szwn@gmail.com. By helping you have a chance to help someone change their life, plus you get a unique t-shirt!

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work is done in german. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a short meeting before workshop to go through the curriculum. Email us
